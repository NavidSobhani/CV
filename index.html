About

Warm, focused, and always improving

I’m passionate about healthcare and learning how science and practical skills come together to help people. I genuinely enjoy studying dentistry because it allows me to help people in a very direct and meaningful way. Oral health is a crucial part of overall health, and dentists play an important role in preventing disease, relieving pain, and improving quality of life. Through dentistry, I can make a real difference in people’s daily comfort, confidence, and well-being, d which is what motivates me to keep learning and improving. I’m currently focused on building strong clinical knowledge and improving my skills for the future.

Every now and then, I work part-time, sometimes in dentistry-related jobs and sometimes in completely different fields which has taught me flexibility and time management.

My focus

I’m especially interested in the fundamentals that matter most early on: patient communication: in Turkish, English and Persian, learning clinical habits that lead to calm, confident care. I’m motivated by steady progress — small improvements every day.
